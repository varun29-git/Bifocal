### A-001: Mertens versus Maier evidence conflict

Type: Contradiction
Status: Synthesized

Why It Matters:

The central empirical dispute is not whether individual nudges can work. It is whether the literature supports a broad overall effectiveness claim after publication-bias correction.

Evidence:

- Source: `sources/mertens-et-al-2021-the-effectiveness-of-nudging-a-meta-analysis-of-choice-architecture-interventions-across-behavioral.pdf`
  Date: 2021-12-30
  Adds: Finds a positive overall effect while acknowledging publication bias.
- Source: `sources/maier-et-al-2022-no-evidence-for-nudging-after-adjusting-for-publication-bias.pdf`
  Date: 2022-07-19
  Adds: Argues that correcting for publication bias removes evidence for an overall effect.
