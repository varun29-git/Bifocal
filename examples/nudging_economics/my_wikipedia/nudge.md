# Nudge

Status: Stable

## Working Definition

A nudge is a deliberate change to choice architecture that predictably influences behavior without forbidding options or substantially changing economic incentives.

## Why It Matters

This definition separates nudging from mandates, bans, subsidies, taxes, fines, and ordinary persuasion. It is the foundation for judging both effectiveness and ethics.
