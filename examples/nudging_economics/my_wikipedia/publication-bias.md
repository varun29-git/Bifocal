# Publication Bias

Status: Draft

## Working Definition

Publication bias is a distortion in the published evidence base caused by selective publication, reporting, or visibility of positive findings.

## Why It Matters

The nudging example turns on publication bias. One meta-analysis finds a positive overall effect, while a later reanalysis argues that the effect disappears after publication-bias adjustment.
