# Choice Architecture

Status: Stable

## Working Definition

Choice architecture is the design of the environment in which people encounter, compare, and select options.

## Why It Matters

Nudging works through choice architecture. Defaults, option order, salience, reminders, social reference information, and friction can change behavior without removing choices.
