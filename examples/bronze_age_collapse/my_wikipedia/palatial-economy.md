# Palatial Economy

Status: Draft

## Working Definition

A palatial economy is a centralized political and economic system organized around palace institutions, elite administration, surplus extraction, storage, craft production, and long-distance exchange.

## Why It Matters

Several collapse explanations depend on the vulnerability of palatial systems. If a society needs high agricultural productivity, stable trade routes, elite administration, and reliable labor obligations, then climate stress, warfare, migration, or trade disruption can cascade through the system.
