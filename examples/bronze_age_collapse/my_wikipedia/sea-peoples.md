# Sea Peoples

Status: Under Review

## Working Definition

The Sea Peoples are named groups in late second millennium BCE Egyptian and Near Eastern sources that are often linked to conflict, migration, and maritime movement during the Late Bronze Age crisis.

## Why It Matters

The Sea Peoples are one of the most visible explanations for the collapse, but the evidence is uneven. They may have been participants in violence and movement, yet the sources do not support making them the sole or simple cause of the crisis.
